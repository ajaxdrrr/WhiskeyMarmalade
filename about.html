ABOUT WHISKEY MARMALADE

Make The World A Better Place With Creation

Whiskey Marmalade is a London-based production company focused on sharp storytelling backed by captivating visuals. Its goal is to bring fresh, entertaining, and most importantly, meaningful content from its teams in London and Manila to the global stage. The company is founded by multi-award-winning actress, acclaimed screenwriter, film director, and author, Bela Padilla.
